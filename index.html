This page is to demonstrate a regular form but with custom messaging rather than stock 'out of the box' messaging

Benefits of custom form validation messaging:

  • Tailor messaging based on your user audience
  • Add humor or something you think would benefit the UX
  • Steer users to where to find information if the content they need to submit is very specific to your data needs
  • Provide specific examples of what the user should type rather than having a message that broadly state "Incorrect format"
